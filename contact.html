Quick Answers

Common Questions

Do I need an appointment for service?
Walk-ins are welcome for most repairs. For custom builds and bike fitting, booking ahead is recommended.
How does presale work?
Reserve a bike with no upfront payment. We confirm availability and contact you when it arrives — typically 1–2 weeks.
Do you offer test rides?
Yes! In-stock bikes are available for test rides in the parking lot. Bring a helmet or borrow one of ours.
Can you order specific bikes?
Absolutely. We work with multiple suppliers and can special order most brands. Lead time is usually 2–4 weeks.
How long do repairs take?
Most standard repairs are done within 24–48 hours. Flat repairs are often same-day or walk-in. We'll always give you an ETA.
Are group rides free?
Our Saturday morning trail rides are always free. Workshops have a small fee ($15–25) to cover instructor time and materials.
