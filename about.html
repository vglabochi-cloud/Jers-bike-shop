The Story

Started With a Wrench and a Dream

Jer's Bike Shop was founded in 2014 by Jer — a lifelong cyclist who got tired of driving 40 minutes to find a shop that actually knew bikes. He opened a small repair stand in his garage, word spread fast, and the rest is history.

Today, Jer's is a full-service shop offering everything from entry-level commuters to custom performance builds — all backed by the same honest, no-pressure service that's kept our customers coming back for over a decade.
